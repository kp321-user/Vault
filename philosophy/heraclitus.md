## Core ideas
Heraclitus used 'physis' in discussions of change and reality, around 500 BCE. Physis remains a bridge between nature as a thing and nature as a process. Its older sense of growth stays visible in the verb root. That older meaning gives the word its depth. It is not just what a thing is, but how it comes to be.
