# John Locke (1632–1704)

## In plain terms
realist philosopher who came before the push towards idealism
early enlightenment thinker
epistemology - empiricist, similar to Francis Bacon
theory of mind: identity and self
mind is a tabula rasa which becomes filled with knowledge though experience, observation and experimentation
as opposed to cartesian theory that people were born with innate ideas
political philosophy: separation of church and state
life liberty and property
father of classical liberalism
religious tolerance
